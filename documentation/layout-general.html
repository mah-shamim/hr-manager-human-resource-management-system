    System Introduction

    HRM is a modern and responsvie small business management system. It is developed by PHP and Codeginiter framework. It is design and develop for thinking shop, small business, company and any types of business.Here has accounting, management, invoice,user and data analysis.It has many features and functions that make employee management and account related work easy. Accounts, tax, payroll, performance and report can be handled almost simulteneously with this system.

Server requirements

  • Apache or IIS or Nginx
  • PHP version 7.4 => 8.0 (ie. recommended: Any PHP version greater than equal 7.4 and less than 8.0)
  • MySQL version 5.0 or greater (recommended: MySQL 5.5 or greater)

Auto Update

The automatic update is a new feature of HRM. This feature is only applicable for V 4.2 After purchasing the HRM when a new update is uploaded in our update server. You will get a notification to update your system. Then just go to the “Auto Update” page, enter your purchase key, select the version and hit update.

Note: before you update your system, we recommend you to download the data backup by clicking 'Download database' button for safety.
